Privacy Policy

Iftar Ready  ·  Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what information Iftar Ready collects, how it is used, and what it does not do.

Information We Collect

  • Location data – Used to calculate accurate prayer times for your area and to find nearby mosques and halal restaurants. Location is requested only when you use the app and is never stored on our servers.
  • App preferences – Settings such as notification preferences, calculation method, and display options are stored locally on your device using your device's storage. This data never leaves your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No personal accounts or registration required.
  • No names, email addresses, or contact information.
  • No usage analytics or tracking.
  • No advertising identifiers.

Third-Party Services

Iftar Ready uses two external APIs to provide its core functionality:

  • Google Places API – Your approximate location is sent to Google to search for nearby mosques and halal restaurants. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Aladhan API – Your location coordinates are sent to Aladhan to retrieve prayer times for your area. Aladhan is a free, non-commercial prayer times service.

No other third parties receive your data. Your data is not sold, rented, or shared for advertising purposes.

Data Storage

All app settings and preferences are stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. We do not operate any servers or databases that store user data.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, Iftar Ready schedules local notifications on your device for prayer times and iftar reminders. These are processed entirely on-device and do not involve any external servers.

Children's Privacy

Iftar Ready does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at contact@meliolabs.io.
